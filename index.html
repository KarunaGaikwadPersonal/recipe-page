Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Mix eggs with salt and pepper. Add water/milk for fluffiness.
  2. Heat the pan: Add butter or oil to a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour eggs and tilt to coat evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): Sprinkle fillings when edges set but center slightly runny.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette, cook 1 more minute, then serve hot.
  6. Enjoy: Season with extra salt or pepper if needed.
